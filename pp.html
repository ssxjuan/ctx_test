Our Privacy Policy

‘Centrex’ is brand registered under ‘TrueChain Commercial Brokers LLC.’ whose main office is located in - Binary Tower, Marasi Drive, Business Bay, Dubai - U.A.E. The privacy policy of Centrex is applied adhering to the regulations and norms as enforced by the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (ob) of subsection (2) of section 87 read with section 43A of the Information Technology Act, 2000 (21 of 2000). The rules under which are called the Information Technology (Reasonable Security practices and procedures and sensitive personal data or information) Rules, 2011. Centrex does not collect data to add value to any of its revenue models. The main reason in doing so is to comply with the rules stated under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act,2002 (PMLA) which includes Know your Customer(KYC) registration.

I. Types of Information Collected by Centrex

1.1 Personal Information submitted during Registration

(a) Full Name

(b) Email Address

(c) Mobile phone number

(d) KYC Documents-Government issued identity card, passport, Bank details, Bank statement etc.

1.2 Device information from which the site has been accessed (Browser name, OS info, IP address, smart phone details etc).

1.3 Web Browser Cookies (Centrex may use cookes on your web browser depending on your browser settings).

II. Why we collect the Information

We maintain strict compliance in accordance with the 'Guidance For a Risk Based Approach To Virtual Assets and Virtual Asset Service Providers' put forward by the Financial Action Task Force (FATF 2019).

2.1 To authenticate identity

We use the information collected to deliver our services and verify user identity. We use the IP address and unique identifiers stored in your device’s cookies to help us authenticate your identity to ensure the legitimacy of the user. Given our legal obligations and system requirements, we cannot provide you with services without data identification, contact information and transaction-related information.

2.2 To protect our users

We use the information collected to protect our platform, users’ accounts and archives. We use IP addresses and cookie data to protect against automated abuse such as spam, phishing and Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks.We analyse trading activity with the goal of detecting suspicious behavior early to prevent potential fraud and loss of funds caused due to bad actors.

2.3 To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

To complying with regulations, governmental requests and user-generated inquiries. We will not disclose or provide any personal information to third party vendors, without review from our legal case team and/or prior consent from the user. Based on diiferent criteria of the user and the transaction, a risk based approach is applied to monitor any occurance of suspiscious activity. This remains a definitive measure to aide in the global effort to combat money laundering, terrorist financing and the financing of proliferation, and other related threats to the integrity of the finacial system. More information about this is provided in detail in the document -'Centrex FATF Guidelines-2019'.

2.4 To measure site performance

We actively measure and analyse data to understand how our services are used. This review activity is conducted by our operations team to continually improve our platform’s performance and to resolve issues with the user experience. We continuously monitor our systems’ activity information and communications with users to look for and quick fix problems.

2.5 To communicate with you

We use personal information collected, like an email address to interact with users directly when providing customer support on a ticket or to keep you informed on log-in details, transactions, and security. Without processing your personal information for confirming each communication, we will not be able to respond to your submitted requests, questions and inquiries. All direct communications are kept confidential and reviewed internally for accuracy.

2.6 To enforce our Terms of Use and other agreements

It is very important for us and our customers, that we continually review, investigate and prevent any potentially prohibited or illegal activities that violate our Terms of Service. For the benefit of our entire user base, we carefully enforce our agreements with third party vendors and actively investigate violations of our posted Terms of Use. Centrex reserves the right to terminate the provision of service to any user found engaging in activities that violate our Terms of Use.

3.3 We will not be liable for the following:

(a) Breach by third party users. In an event where such a breach is due, not by our negligence or non-compliance of the law.

(b) Any appropriation of data pursuant requested from any governmental authority or any court.
