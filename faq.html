General Questions

I. What are trading pairs?

An asset pair is the quotation of the relative value of an asset unit against the unit of another asset in a crypto asset exchange market. The asset that is used as the reference is called the quote asset and the asset that is quoted in relation is called the base asset.

III. What are crypto currencies?

Cryptocurrencies are cryptographically secured digital Assets that can be circulated using protocols in a network.

These assets can be circulated and used as a mode of payment. In turn they have a real usable economic value and can be exchanged with the mainstream fiat equivalent. In Centrex crypto currencies are a type of Digital Assets that can be pruchase sold or transferred.

IV. What is a Digital Asset?

A digital asset is generally anything that is created and stored digitally, is identifiable and discoverable, and has or provides value. Digital assets have become more popular and valuable as technological advances become integrated into our personal and professional lives. Data, images, video, written content, and more have long been considered digital assets with ownership rights.

V. What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is the term used to define a technology that encapsulates a cryptographically secured distributed ledger system spread across a network consisting of nodes. The ledger system can be modified by the nodes on the network but will only be validated if it is in unison with the previous entries of the ledger and is approved by the majority of the nodes on the network. Nodes may consist of any point of access contributing to the functioning of the network. They may be in the form of desk top computers, laptops, phones, servers or independent GPU's.

VI. What is a protocol?

The set of rules or instructions that define the channel of communication on a network is called protocol. It defines the foundation layer of the network. General examples of protocol are http, torrent etc. Protocols defining block chains are bit coin, etherium, ripple etc.

V. How to Trade?

Trading digital assets has never been more easy. First choose the type of order you'd like to execute.

Market Order

In Market order the price of the trade is determined by the market automatically. It can be executed as demostrated in the following steps.

A. Choose the currency pair to be traded.

B. Select a Buy or Sell order.

C. Enter the amount required to be traded.

D. Confirm Trade

Limit Order

In limit order the price of the trade can be manually set. It can be executed as demostrated in the following steps.

A. Select the currency pair to be traded.

B. Select Buy or Sell order.

C. Enter the amount to be traded.

D. Set the price of the trade.

E. Confirm Trade.
